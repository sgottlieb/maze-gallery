# Credits

## Dollz art

The `Dollz` texture in the Retro series is a composition of 16 pixel dolls sampled from four galleries (Preppy, Emo/Scene, Fantasy, Wonderkins) on [Dollzmania](https://dollzmania.neocities.org/galleries/) — a community archive preserving early-2000s pixel doll art. The individual dolls were originally created by anonymous artists on the early-2000s dollz scene (DollzMania, PrinzessKittyDollz, and countless other long-gone GeoCities / Angelfire / Xanga hubs). They are included here as a respectful homage to that era and art form.

If you are an original doll artist who would like a piece removed or credited specifically, contact Sara at sarag@liminalgardens.com.

## Procedurally-generated textures

The other Retro textures (`Brick`, `Sky`, `Checker`, `Stars`) are procedurally generated in `scripts/make-retro.html` — no outside sources.
