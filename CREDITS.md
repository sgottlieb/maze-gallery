# Credits

## Procedurally-generated textures

The Retro textures (`Brick`, `Sky`, `Checker`, `Stars`) are procedurally generated in `scripts/make-retro.html` — no outside sources.

## Art collections

The Match, Me, Mother, and Animated series are all original works by Sara Gottlieb (© Sara Gottlieb).

## Technology

- [Three.js](https://threejs.org/) r170 — 3D rendering
- Maze generation: recursive backtracking (standard algorithm)
- Hosted as a static site
